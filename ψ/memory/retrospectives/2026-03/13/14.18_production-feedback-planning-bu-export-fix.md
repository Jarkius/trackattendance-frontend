The user's patience in refining rather than just approving a mediocre plan shows real engineering discipline.

## Lessons Learned
- **Pattern**: User domain expertise > AI technical proposals. The user's rejection of random delay in favor of central camera was a fundamentally better architecture. Listen to production operators.
- **Pattern**: Cross-cutting features (Live Sync) need mode-level thinking, not feature-level. Batch sync vs real-time is a system mode, not a checkbox.
- **Discovery**: The `DUPLICATE_BADGE_ACTION` warn/block setting is a reusable pattern for any kind of validation — local or cloud-based.
